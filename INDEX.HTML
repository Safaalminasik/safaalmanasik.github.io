About Us

More Than a Travel Agency — A Spiritual Companion

Safa Al Manasik Travel & Tours was founded with a singular vision — to make the sacred journeys of Hajj and Umrah accessible, comfortable, and spiritually fulfilling for every Muslim family in Pakistan.

Based in the heart of Lahore, we have grown from a small office to one of the most trusted names in pilgrimage travel. Our team personally accompanies groups, ensuring every detail — from hotel proximity to Haram to guided Ziyarat — is perfected. We also specialize in international tourism and visa services for destinations worldwide.
